A max scan runtime may be provided so that the length of time the CI process takes to provide feedback can be controlled.
  • This option is ignored if 'Scan has been submitted' has been selected as the build advance indicator
  • This option is ignored if 'Scan has been started' has been selected as the build advance indicator
  • The time limit will begin to take affect when the scan moves into scanning state
  • Upon reaching the time limit, the in progress scan will be stopped and the build will advance as normal

The following format must be used for defining a max wait time: 
       0d 5h 30m
  • (d) - Days - The time in days to wait
  • (h) - Hours - The time in hours to wait
  • (m) - Minutes- The time in minutes to wait
A quantity and unit must be supplied for each of the above. e.g.
  • 1 day: 1d 0h 0m
  • 5 hours: 0d 5h 0m
  • 3 hours, 30 minutes: 0d 3h 30m
